Google Ads for Apartments

Google Ads for Apartments

How can you use Google Ads to amplify your property listings online?

As a property management company, finding high-quality tenants for your available property listings is a top priority. More than ever, potential tenants are using the internet to help find a new place to call home. To help make sure your listings are in front of your target tenants, we recommend using pay-per-click advertising through Google Ads. Google Ads appear right at the top of the search engine results page.

Google Ads operates on a pay-per-click (PPC) strategy, meaning you only pay for the ad once someone has clicked it. This ensures that you only pay for potential tenants who click and are interested. We have landing pages that are tested and proven to convert potential tenants into applicants. Contrary to other forms of online advertising, pay-per-click Google Ads does not charge you for the number of people who view your ad but take no further action. So thousands of potential tenants could see your ad, but you only pay for the ones who click. Think of it like an online billboard that potential tenants can take action with.
